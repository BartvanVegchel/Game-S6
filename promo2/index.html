Our Idea

Kids nowadays aren't getting enough exercise. One of the biggest reasons is gaming. With that in mind, we are currently developing a game that can only be played through real life movement! Kids have to get off the couch and start moving to improve in the game.

  • The problem
  • Our solution
  • Why we need your help
  • Our progress

The problem

80% of children lack the daily movement they need to be healthy. One of the reasons for their lack of healthy movement is the fact that a lot of children like to play videogames. Often they like to play their videogames more then they do moving around. Videogames can be very addictive. That's why we aim to make our game a healthy addiction.

Our solution

We are making a game that trades physical movement for energy points. Daily activities such as walking, cycling and running gives you energy points. Energy points allow you to play the game. You can use them to explore new worlds and collect all monsters!

Why we need your help

We need some help getting our game out there! So we are asking you to help us out! The funds we hope to collect will mainly be used for promotional purposes. We would like to get our game out to the world, because we are convinced Moving Monsters is a great way to help kids get more active.

Our progress

We are currently working hard on developing the game and creating an experience children love. To do this, we have asked numerous kids for feedback. From feedback for design to the complete game experience. This feedback has been very valuable to us and we plan to use their input throughout the developmental process. We plan to have the game up and running on the 20th of may 2016.
