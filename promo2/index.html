Our Idea

Kids nowadays aren't getting enough exercise. One of the biggest reasons is gaming. With that in mind, we are currently developing a game that can only be played through real life movement! Kids have to get off the couch and start moving to improve in the game.

  • The problem
  • Our solution
  • Why we need your help
  • Our progress

The problem

80% of children lack the daily movement they need to be healthy growing up.
One of the reasons for their lack of healthy movement is the fact that a lot of children like to play videogames.
Often times they like to play their videogames more then they do moving around on the playground.
Videogames can be very addictive, we want to make it a healthy addiction.

Our solution

We will make game that will trade movement for energy points.
Daily activities such as walking, cycling and running all help you gather energy points.
These energy points will help you play the game.
You can trade the them for exploration of new areas and collect all the monsters!

Why we need your help

We would need some help getting our game out there!
So we are asking you to help us out!
The funds we hope to collect will be used for promotional purposes.
We would like to get our game out in the world, because we are convinced Moving Monsters is a healthy videogame.

Our progress

We are currently working hard on developing the game and creating an experience children would combine namely, moving and playing.
To do this we have asked numerous kids for input from design to the complete game experience.
This input has been very valuable to us and we plan to use their input throughout the developmental process.
We plan to have the game up and running on the 20th of may 2016

Moving Monsters, The app
